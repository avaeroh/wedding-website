Our Story

Rachael and Jonathan first crossed paths in Brighton when their collegue/friend/matchmaker Callum introduced them to another at a work event.

In the following few months, Jon and Rach realised they have a lot in common, including a love of fitness, exploring London, and wearing tank-tops.

Just over two years later, one of which was spent in a tiny room together, Jonathan finally succame to Stockholm Syndrome and proposed to Rachael.

On Saturday 18th September Jon took Rachael and Faye for a lovely morning walk along the Thames. As they got further along the route, they found a small path leading down to a beach that had revealed itself in the low tied. It was here that they decided to set up a breakfast picnic with their kitten Faye, and watched the sunrise. It's there that Jon asked Rachael if he could take a photo of Rach and their cat with the warm beach backdrop. As Rachael and Faye stood by the water watching the sunrise reflect off the Thames, Jon walked over and proceeded to get down on one knee... and of course Rachael said YES!
