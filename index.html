Our Story

Rachael and Jonathan first crossed paths in Brighton when their mutual friend Callum convinced Rachael to wait in the hotel until Jon arrived so they could all walk to the company event together.

It was after this initial meeting that Jon and Rachael started talking more and discovered that we had a mutual love of cake, doughnuts, fitness and exploring London.

Just over 2 years, one pandemic and one Faye later, Jonathan decided to propose to Rachael!.

On Saturday 18th September Jon took Rachael and Faye for a lovely morning walk along the thames. As we got further along the walk we found a cute little path that led down to the beach. It was here we decided to set up a breakfast picnic with Faye to watch the sunrise. But first Jon wanted to get a cute picture of Rachael and Faye by the water. As Rachael and Faye stood by the water watching the sunrise reflect off the thames, Jon came over and got down on one knee. And of course Rachael said YES!
